We are one of Northern Michigan’s premiere excavators of old septic tanks and diggers of holes for new ones. We also excavate ground and dig for new septic drainage lines and new leach field trenches.

Septic Tank Removal

We are one of Northern Michigan’s premiere excavators of old septic tanks and diggers of holes for new ones. We also excavate ground and dig for new septic drainage lines and new leach field trenches.

If your old septic system stops working we have the equipment, man power and experience to remove it safely and with minimal disruption to the surrounding landscape. Proper removal of broker, abandoned or disused septic tanks is important to avoid caving in, falling in or the collapse of the septic tank hole.
